Sustainability

Hello dear friends and family!

Sustainability is one of our core values, and we hope you will help us make our celebration as environmentally sustainable as possible. Here are four actions that we hope you will take!

The fashion industry produces 10% of all humanity’s carbon emissions and is the second-largest consumer of the world’s water supply [1]. The fashion industry is currently responsible for more annual carbon emissions than all international flights and maritime shipping combined [2]. This is not to speak of the human cost of fashion: gender-based violence is rampant against female garment workers [3]. Please help us avoid these egregious human and environmental impacts by reusing your existing wardrobe, borrowing an outfit from a friend, or thrifting a pre-loved outfit.

We are grateful to have family and friends coming to the wedding from across the country. In light of the environmental impact of flying, we suggest that long-distance guests consider purchasing a carbon offset for their travel. In addition, we recommend carpooling to all wedding events; please let us know if you would like help coordinating rides!

Unfortunately, the Saratoga Country Club is only able to offer disposable plastic cups during our outdoor ceremony because they have an indoor-only glassware policy, and we are unable to acquire reusable cups for Sangeet. Please help us minimize waste by bringing a reusable water bottle to Sangeet and, if you think you'll be thirsty, the Ceremony! After the Ceremony, you will have an opportunity to drop it off in your vehicle before heading into the reception.

Your presence at our wedding is a present enough, especially if you are joining us from out of state! If you would like to gift us something, we offer our heartfelt thanks and request that instead of a material gift, you gift us an experience or send a financial gift via Venmo. This is also important because we live in Boston and will have no easy way of bringing material gifts back across the country with us.
